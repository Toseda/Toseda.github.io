What is Gacha?

The term "Gacha" comes from the Japanese toy dispensers called Gashapon(gachapon), where users roll for a random item from the machine. This was shortened to "gacha", and became the catch-all term for all anime style mobile games where gambling is the main method of obtaining new characters.
