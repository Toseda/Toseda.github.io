What games do we cover here? See for yourself!

Genshin Impact

After being separated from your sibling, you find yourself on alone on a beach, stepping into the wide world of Teyvat explore years of content and open world exploration in this Action RPG, featuring 100+ playable characters and tons of content.

Honkai: Star Rail

Awoken with no memory of who you were, you take a step aboard the Astral Express to see the cosmos and begin your new journey. Offers tens of hours of story content currently, with a turn based battle system and sci-fi theme.

Arknights: Endfield

The most recent of the games on this site, Endfield offers unique factory management gameplay alongside offering an indepth combat system around skill points as you take the role of the Endmin and experience the story of the planet Talos II.
